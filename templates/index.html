PlantPal

A place for plants

About

Many plant owners agree, although they love their seedlings and saplings they do miss the occasional watering. Life is busy and you need help with watering your plants. That's why we created PlantPal. We provide you with an easy to use reminder system of which plants you need to water and how often. Pick your plants and the frequency of watering will begin calculating automatically. Each time you log in or whenever you have exceeded the days you need to water your plant, PlantPal will let you know it's time. That way you can focus on growing your beautiful garden, and your plants can focus on growing.
